Fresh Greek Salad

Prep Time: 15 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Cucumber and Tomatoes
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olives
  • Olive Oil & Oregano

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables into chunks.
  2. Mix in a large bowl.
  3. Top with feta and olives.
  4. Drizzle with oil and herbs.
