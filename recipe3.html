Roasted Tomato Soup

Prep Time: 45 Mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 6 Large Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 cups Vegetable Broth
  • Fresh Basil

Instructions

  1. Roast tomatoes and onion at 200°C for 30 mins.
  2. Blend vegetables until smooth.
  3. Add broth and simmer in a pot.
  4. Garnish with basil.
