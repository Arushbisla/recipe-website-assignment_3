Creamy Tomato Pasta

Prep Time: 20 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in a pan.
  3. Add sauce and cream, then simmer.
  4. Toss in pasta and serve.
