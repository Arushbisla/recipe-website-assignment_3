Fluffy Pancakes

Prep Time: 15 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 2 tbsp Sugar

Instructions

  1. Whisk dry and wet ingredients separately.
  2. Combine until just mixed.
  3. Pour batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Flip when bubbles appear.
