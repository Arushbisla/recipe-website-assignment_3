Quick Beef Tacos

Prep Time: 25 Mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Taco Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Shredded Lettuce and Cheese

Instructions

  1. Brown the beef in a skillet.
  2. Add seasoning and a splash of water.
  3. Warm the taco shells.
  4. Fill shells with meat and toppings.
